Hybrid Festivals


An online counterpart of a festival does not necessarily turn it into a hybrid festival. It is the technological advancements that enhance the experience. These technological advancements are then building blocks that can fully immerse the festival visitors. Interactions with these advancements it is important to get right and that also comes with a customer journey.

Dutch Rose Media gave me the opportunity to work on these technological advancements and to experiment with them to get a better grasp on what the story is behind hybrid festivals. I will be starting with a continuation of a group project from the previous semester, this is called the Body Projection Mapping. After that, I will a working alongside the development of a building block that will change how people can experience live performances. Toward the end of my internship, I will be working alongside another existing product that will also enhance the overall festival experience.
